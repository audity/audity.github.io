title: Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:
- Chicken thighs (skin on preferred)
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tbspn unsalted buter
- 3 cloves butter
- 1 tbspn brown sugar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 tspn dried thyme
- 1 tspn dried oregano
- 1 lb green beans

Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 400F
2. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper
3. Melt 1 tbspn butter on an ovenproof skillet over medium. Add the chicken and sear on both sides until golden.
4. Remove the chicken thighs and set aside. 
5. Add the garlic and stir. Add the brown sugar, honey, thyme and oregano. Stir to combine and reduce heat.
6. Return the chicken to the skillet and coat in sauce. 
7. Add the green beans
8. Bake for 25 minutes
